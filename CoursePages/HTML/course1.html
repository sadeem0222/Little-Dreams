Course Goals
  • 1. Vowels and Consonants Mastery:
    • Recognize and distinguish between vowels and consonants.
    • Use mnemonic tricks to remember the vowels and consonants effectively.
  • 2. Understanding Silent E:
    • Comprehend the role of Silent E in changing the pronunciation of words.
    • Identify words where Silent E is used and understand its impact on pronunciation.
  • 3. Noun Recognition:
    • Learn and identify nouns as naming words.
    • Classify common nouns and proper nouns in sentences.
  • 4. Demonstrative Pronouns:
    • Understand the usage of "this" and "that" as demonstrative pronouns.
    • Apply the knowledge in describing objects and situations.
  • 5. Articles A, An, and The:
    • Grasp the concept of articles and their usage in sentences.
    • Formulate sentences using the correct article for a given context.
  • 6. Pronouns Exploration:
    • Introduce and recognize pronouns suitable for kids.
    • Apply pronouns in sentences, replacing nouns for clarity and variety.
  • 7. Differentiating Common and Proper Nouns:
    • Differentiate between common nouns and proper nouns.
    • Practice identifying and categorizing nouns appropriately.
  • 8. Understanding Linking Verbs:
    • Recognize linking verbs in sentences.
    • Comprehend their role in connecting the subject to the rest of the sentence.
  • 9. Tense Awareness:
    • Learn and differentiate between past, present, and future tenses.
    • Apply the knowledge to construct sentences in different tenses.
  • 10. Application of Language Rules:
    • Apply the learned concepts in practical language exercises.
    • Engage in activities that reinforce understanding and usage.
